1.1. Hi human, I am Den, I am an engineer by training and by trade and recently I have become increasingly interested in the question of human experience and its interconnection with technologies. As we are nearing or arguably witnessing the nascent of a new species (AI) on Earth, the question of better understanding our own species and how to adapt to inevitable changes that technologies bring at the increasing pace is as urgent as ever.
For the last couple of months I have been working on the project that I call PURR.

PURR is a community of humans striving to love themselves
and settling for no less than tolerating themselves.

PURR is a series of interactive experiences for humans that according to a dataset of personally conducted experiments help to create an open dialog about who human is, what human needs and wants, and what does it all mean if anything.

As a first step to showcase my ideas and invite humans to participate I am creating PURR Platform online.

The community building element is at the very core of PURR project.
